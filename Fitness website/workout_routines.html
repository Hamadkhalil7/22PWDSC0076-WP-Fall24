Full Body Workout

Description: This full body workout targets all major muscle groups and is suitable for all fitness levels.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 8 reps

Upper Body Strength Training

Description: This workout focuses on strengthening the upper body, including arms, shoulders, and back.

Exercises:

  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Seated Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps

Cardio Blast

Description: A high-energy cardio workout designed to get your heart pumping and burn calories.

Exercises:

  • Jump Rope: 5 minutes
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Cool Down Walk: 5 minutes
