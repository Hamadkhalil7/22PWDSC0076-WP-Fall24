Weight Loss Diet

This diet focuses on creating a calorie deficit while ensuring you get the necessary nutrients to stay healthy. It emphasizes whole foods, lean proteins, and plenty of fruits and vegetables.

Recommended Foods:

  • Lean meats (chicken, turkey, fish)
  • Fruits (berries, apples, oranges)
  • Vegetables (spinach, broccoli, carrots)
  • Whole grains (quinoa, brown rice, oats)
  • Healthy fats (avocados, nuts, olive oil)

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with berries and a sprinkle of nuts
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette
  • Snack: Sliced apple with almond butter
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with steamed broccoli and quinoa

Weight Gain Diet

This diet is designed to help you gain weight in a healthy way by increasing your calorie intake with nutrient-dense foods. It focuses on high-calorie foods that provide essential nutrients.

Recommended Foods:

  • Nut butters (peanut butter, almond butter)
  • Whole grains (whole grain bread, pasta, rice)
  • Dairy products (milk, cheese, yogurt)
  • Healthy oils (olive oil, coconut oil)
  • Protein sources (eggs, lean meats, legumes)

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with banana, protein powder, and peanut butter
  • Lunch: Turkey sandwich on whole grain bread with cheese and avocado
  • Snack: Trail mix with nuts and dried fruit
  • Dinner: Stir-fried beef with vegetables served over rice
